NISSAN SKYLINE GT-R R34

Welcome to the World of Skyline GTR R34!

Fasten your seatbelts and brace yourself for a journey into the world of one of the most iconic cars ever made - the Nissan Skyline GTR R34. With a lineage dating back to 1957, the Skyline has transformed through the years to become the beast that the R34 is.

The Beast from the East

Produced between 1999 and 2002, the R34 was the worthy culmination of the Skyline range. Armed with a turbocharged 2.6L engine pushing out a massive 276 horsepower, and equipped with the ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system, it took the automobile world by storm. Its striking design and formidable performance won it a cult-like following among car enthusiasts around the world. It's not just a car, it's a statement.
